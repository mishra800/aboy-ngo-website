ABOUT ABYO

The AAKANDA BHARATHA Foundation stands as a beacon of hope and catalyst for change in the communities we serve. Established on October 31, 2016, and formally registered on October 31, 2019, in Chilkanagar, Uppal, Hyderabad, our foundation embodies the spirit of compassion, dedication, and service. Over the past eight years, we have emerged as a leading force in social welfare, championing causes that uplift the marginalized, empower the youth, and foster sustainable development.

Mission

Driven by a deep-rooted commitment to social justice and equality, our multifaceted initiatives span education, healthcare, livelihood empowerment, environmental conservation, and disaster relief. From organizing medical camps in remote villages to providing scholarships for underprivileged students, our endeavors touch the lives of individuals across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, transcending geographical boundaries

Vision

At the core of our foundation lies a passionate team of over 650 youth members and 70 active teams in Hyderabad and 12 districts, driven by a shared vision of creating positive change. Our approach is holistic, addressing not just immediate needs but also advocating for long-term systemic solutions to systemic challenges.

The mission of The AAKANDA BHARATHA Foundation is to be a driving force for social transformation and community empowerment. Our relentless pursuit of this mission is guided by our unwavering commitment to serving humanity with integrity, compassion, and excellence.

Empower Youth: We believe in the potential of youth as agents of change. Through mentorship, skill development programs, and leadership initiatives, we empower young people to become catalysts for positive social change.
