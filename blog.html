Blankets Distribution

Our mission is simple: to provide warmth and comfort to those in need. As temperatures drop, many individuals and families in our community struggle to stay warm, facing the harsh realities of homelessness, poverty, or other challenging circumstances. Through our Blankets Distribution Campaign, we aim to alleviate some of this hardship by distributing blankets to those who need them most.
